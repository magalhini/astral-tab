Nótt Dagr 🌞 🌙

In Norse Mythology, Dagr is the personification of day. Nótt, the personification of night.

Nótt Dagr is a pet project by Ricardo Magalhães that shows your location's dawn, sunrise, sunset and dusk times. It was built as an excuse to experiment with the Geolocation API and suncalc.

It was originally designed to work as a Chrome Tab extension (soon!), and it's freely available on Github.

More themes and updates soon to come. Say hi on Twitter!
